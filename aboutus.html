About Us

Brian's Bean Shop is a small business that was founded in 2003 by a man named Brian Bean. Brian Bean was a 23 year old office clerk living in the heart of New York City. One day Brian went on a business trip to Boston, Massachusetts, and during the short time there, fell in love with the bean-oriented community and business. Brian Bean left his job as an office clerk and moved to Hawaii to pursue a new life as a bean distributer. Brian's Bean Shop started off slow but has gained much traction in recent years.

Our Goal

Our beans are grown in Argentinas extremely fertile soil, nurtured with Earths most nutrition rich water and grown by the finest farmers of the century. After the beans are hand-picked, they are shipped to a facility in Texas where it is cleaned, checked for defects, and finally shiped to the customers home. Brian's Bean Shop's goal is to provide our customers with the absolute highest quality of beans and ultimately to leave them satisfied with our products.
